Negative Sentence Rules

do not

Use with I, you, we, they in present tense.
Example: We do not watch TV at lunch.

does not

Use with he, she, it, or one person in present tense.
Example: Elaine does not do homework at recess.

did not

Use with all subjects in past tense.
Example: Kobe did not read on Monday.
