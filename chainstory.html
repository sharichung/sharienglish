🦸‍♂️ The Chain Story Game 🦸‍♀️

Interactive ESL Storytelling for 1-on-1 Zoom Lessons

📚 🎨 🦄 🚀

👩‍🏫 Teacher Note: This game is designed for you to control while screen sharing. Your student will participate by speaking or choosing options verbally.

🎯

Learning Goals

  • ✔️ Practice creative writing
  • ✔️ Use grammar in context
  • ✔️ Build vocabulary
  • ✔️ Develop speaking skills
🎮

How to Play

  1. 1️⃣ Choose a story theme
  2. 2️⃣ Take turns adding sentences
  3. 3️⃣ Follow the mission cards
  4. 4️⃣ Complete the story together
  5. 5️⃣ Read and reflect on your story
    6. ⚙️

    Game Features

    • 🌟 Story starter prompts
    • 🌟 Mission cards for guidance
    • 🌟 Word banks for support
    • 🌟 Fun visuals and animations
    • 🌟 Final story display
