How to Play 🎮

1. Choose Random Mode (surprise letters) or Alphabetical Mode (A to Z order)

2. Look at the Mission Board to see which letter to rescue

3. Student shouts the target letter out loud

4. Teacher clicks the correct balloon to help the superhero

5. Collect stars and complete levels to become a Letter Hero!

🎯 Goal: Rescue all letters and save the day!
